================================================================================
THE ARCHITECTURE (Simple)
================================================================================

Message Flow:
  Candidate sends WhatsApp
    ↓
  Webhook receives → resolveCandidate → AI reply → Send via Twilio
    ↓
  postMessage() decides: Portal only OR Portal + WhatsApp

Smart Delivery (postMessage):
  Is candidate logged in? (lastActiveAt < 15 min)
    ├─ YES  → Deliver to portal only (no WhatsApp)
    └─ NO   → Check opt-in + 24h window → Send WhatsApp
